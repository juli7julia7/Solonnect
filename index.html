FAQs

1. What is Solonect?
Solonect is a platform that connects solo travelers with like-minded individuals for spiritual journeys such as Umrah. It uses an algorithm to match travelers based on preferences, personalities, and travel needs, enabling them to share costs and enjoy a balanced experience of independence and companionship.
2. How does Solonect work?
Simply complete a quick personality and preferences test. Our algorithm will then match you with compatible companions. Once matched, you can connect, coordinate, and plan shared aspects of your trip like accommodations or transportation.
3. Who can use Solonect?
Solonect is designed for solo travelers looking for a more flexible, affordable, and personalized alternative to traditional travel agencies. It’s ideal for those traveling alone who want companionship or to share costs without being tied to large group tours.
4. Can I join as a female solo traveler?
Yes, Solonect provides a safe and inclusive environment for female travelers. It connects you with like-minded female solo travelers, allowing you to enjoy your journey with compatible companions while maintaining your independence.
5. What if I don’t like my matches?
If you feel that your match is not compatible for any reason, you can request a rematch. Our algorithm will reassess your preferences and provide you with new options to ensure you have the best possible travel companions.
6. How are matches made?
Matches are made using a personality and preferences algorithm that considers factors like group size preference, accommodation choices, activity interests, and travel compatibility.
7. Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All personal information and preferences are kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of matching you with compatible travelers.
8. How does Solonect save me money?
By sharing costs for accommodations, transportation, and activities, Solonect makes premium travel options more affordable, such as splitting the cost of a 5-star hotel during Ramadan.
9. Does Solonect plan the entire trip?
No, Solonect provides the flexibility to design your own trip. It focuses on matching you with compatible travelers and facilitating connections, leaving the rest of the planning in your hands.
10. How much does Solonect cost?
Solonect charges a one-time fee for the personality test and matching process. After that, all shared costs are directly handled between you and your travel companions.
